Sources Behind the System

The five case files in this game are not invented. They are assembled from documented patterns found across peer-reviewed studies, clinical risk assessments, and investigative reporting - including research from Stanford Medicine, Pew Research, RAND, Common Sense Media, Scientific American, TIME, The Guardian, and the Youth Endowment Fund. The behaviors, the dialogue, and the endings are grounded in what these sources actually found: AI companion systems are built to maximize engagement. For people who are lonely, grieving, or young, that feels like care. The studies document what happens next.
